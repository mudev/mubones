Mu Flex

Mu (Japanese/Korean) and Wu (Chinese) is a word which has been roughly translated as "no","none","null","without" and "no meaning". While used mainly in Japanese or Chinese as a prefix to imply the absense of something, in English it is more famously used as a response to certain koans and other questions in zen buddhism, intending to indicate that the question itself was wrong.
